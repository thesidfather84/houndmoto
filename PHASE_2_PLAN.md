## Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: Data Accuracy
**Potential Impact:** Bad specs lose user trust  
**Mitigation:**
- Require 2 sources minimum (manual + online database)
- Add disclaimer: "Verify against owner's manual"
- User feedback form (flag incorrect specs)
- Monthly accuracy audit

### Risk 2: Slow Data Collection
**Potential Impact:** Miss timeline, fall behind competitors  
**Mitigation:**
- Hire automotive data specialist (contract: $3K/month)
- Crowdsource specs from mechanic community
- Auto-scrape verified specs from dealer sites

### Risk 3: Ad Network Approval
**Potential Impact:** Months without revenue  
**Mitigation:**
- Apply to Google AdSense week 1
- Backup: Mediavine or Ezoic
- Launch affiliate immediately (no approval needed)

### Risk 4: Competitor Response
**Potential Impact:** Existing competitors improve offerings  
**Mitigation:**
- First-mover advantage on specs database (already ahead)
- Build community/reviews (harder to copy)
- Partner with shops (switching cost)
