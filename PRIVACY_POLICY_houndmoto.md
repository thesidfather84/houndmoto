## 7. Cookies and Tracking

### 7.1 How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for:

- **Session management:** Keeping you logged in (if applicable)
- **Analytics:** Understanding usage patterns
- **Preferences:** Remembering your choices

### 7.2 Types of Cookies

- **Session cookies:** Deleted when you close your browser
- **Persistent cookies:** Remain for longer periods
- **Third-party cookies:** From analytics services (Google Analytics)

### 7.3 Controlling Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser:

- **Chrome:** Settings → Privacy → Cookies
- **Firefox:** Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
- **Safari:** Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
- **Edge:** Settings → Privacy → Cookies

**Disabling cookies may affect Service functionality.**
